## Coordinate System:
This is how positions (coordinates) work on Teng's two dimensional grids.  
Townly makes use of the `Position()` class, ensuring no position can ever be negative.

```m

    0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
    ---------------  X/W
0 | o   o
1 |   o o   o   o
2 |   o   o o o   o
3 |     o   o
4 | o           o

 Y/H

```
